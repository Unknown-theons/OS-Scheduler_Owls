About Priority Scheduling

Priority Scheduling is an algorithm that assigns CPU based on process priorities. In this scheme:

  • Each process is assigned a priority value
  • Process with highest priority is executed first
  • Can be preemptive or non-preemptive
  • May lead to starvation of low-priority processes
  • Often used in real-time systems and operating systems
